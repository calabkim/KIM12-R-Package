1. Yacht Hydrodynamics Dataset

Description:

This dataset explores the hydrodynamic performance of sailing yachts based on various ship design parameters.

Access:

https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Yacht+Hydrodynamics

2. QSAR Fish Toxicity Dataset

Description:

Quantitative structure activity relationship (QSAR) model predicting fish toxicity based on chemical descriptors.

Access:

https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/QSAR+fish+toxicity

3. Concrete Compressive Strength Dataset

Description:

This dataset helps in assessing the compressive strength of concrete blends, considering different ingredients.

Access:

https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Concrete+Compressive+Strength
